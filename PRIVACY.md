# Privacy Policy for Likes to Go

Last updated: 2026-06-24

Likes to Go saves your SoundCloud likes as a backup file. Your data stays on your device and is not sent to external servers.

## What Data Is Handled

Likes to Go reads SoundCloud likes track information. This **data is used only to build the backup file** you choose to save.

## Downloads

Likes to Go **saves a file only after you choose** to save an export. You control the file saved to your device. Audio files are never downloaded.

## External Services

Likes to Go **does not use external servers**, analytics, telemetry, ads, payment processing, or third-party tracking.

## Data Sharing

Likes to Go **does not sell, share, or transmit your data** to third parties.

## Changes

This policy will be updated if data practices change.
